OUR STORY

Ozan Karabulut and Om Vadodaria were inseparable since childhood. Both were fascinated by cars because of how complex and life-changing one invention truly was. They always promised each other that they would work together for the rest of their lives in the car industry, until Om was hospitalized.

Over the course of three years, Om Vadodaria was in and out of the Emergency Room until the sad day on October 14, 2014, when he passed away. Ozan was devastated that he lost his best friend and that he couldn’t fulfill his promise. He never knew the reason to why the world took his best friend from him, but he later found out that it was because of cars. He thought, how can my love of cars cause my best friend’s death?” From that day, he sought out to create an eco-friendly car with clean emissions and is electric. With that, Ohm Industries was created in dedication to his lifelong friend.

Ohm Industries began in 2015 and has become one of the leading companies in clean cars. It has become a multi-million dollar company in only five years with the goal to prevent anyone from dying due to carbon monoxide poisoning again.
